# Hivoraa

A student platform that transforms a campus into a connected community. Built by a student, for students.

---

## What is Hivoraa?

Hivoraa turns a fragmented campus into a supportive community. No professors, no administration — just students helping each other. The platform has three main spaces and four supporting modules.

---

## The Three Worlds

### Knowledge Square

The academic help feed. A student stuck on an assignment posts a question. Others vote on difficulty and reply with explanations or screenshots. Everything is visible without clicking.

- Ask for help on assignments
- Vote on difficulty (easy, medium, hard)
- Reply with text or images
- Filter by course and search
- See difficulty trends across the class

### Trust Circle

A semi-private space for sensitive topics. Students use temporary nicknames in themed rooms. You can read without posting. Talk about stress, loneliness, family, money, sleep, or relationships.

- 6 themed rooms
- Read anonymously
- Post under a temporary pseudonym

### Listening Chamber

100% anonymous crisis support. No name, no email, no IP stored. Choose a door, write your story, get a unique code. Return later with the code to check for a response.

- 5 doors (financial, mental health, harassment, academic, campus ideas)
- Unique tracking code
- No identity stored

---

## Supporting Modules

### Study Groups

Create or join study groups with real-time chat. Send text or audio messages. Audio is recorded directly in the browser.

### Resources

Share study materials — PDFs, images, video links, or website links. Filter by type and course.

### Talent Wall

Exchange skills. Post what you offer and what you want in return. Public chat under each talent.

### Profile

View your stats. Edit your pseudonym. Manage your posts, groups, resources, and talents.

---

## How Authentication Works

1. Click "Sign in with Google"
2. Choose your Google account
3. You are automatically logged in
4. First-time users choose a pseudonym
5. Your session stays active for 7 days
